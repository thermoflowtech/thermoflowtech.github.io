About ThermoFlow

ThermoFlow Technologies, LLC is based in Raleigh, North Carolina and was founded to translate advances in surface science and thermofluid physics into practical engineering mechanisms.

The company operates at the intersection of surface chemistry, multiphase fluid dynamics, and device-level thermofluid design, with an intentional focus on early-stage mechanism validation to support subsequent development and scale-up efforts.
