Technology Platform

ThermoFlow’s core technology platform is built around engineered wetting and surface-mediated phase-change control. By tailoring surface chemistry and interface design, we enable liquid behaviors that are difficult or impossible to achieve with traditional capillary-, gravity-, or pressure-driven approaches.

Core capabilities

  • Engineered wetting and low-hysteresis surface design
  • Surface-driven droplet transport and shedding
  • Condensation, evaporation, and phase-change enhancement
  • Multiphase-flow modeling and droplet-trajectory analysis
  • Benchtop thermofluid experimentation and prototyping

Execution model

ThermoFlow integrates modeling → surface fabrication → controlled experimentation → iterative refinement. This hybrid approach enables rapid evaluation of surface-enabled mechanisms, identification of dominant physical limits, and early technical risk reduction prior to larger-scale development efforts.
